About Malappuram

Malappuram located in the southern part of former Malabar district, is a revenue district of the Indian state of Kerala. The city of Malappuram, the district headquarters, gives the district its name. It is the most populous district in Kerala, which is home to about 12.3% of the total population of the state.[6] The district was formed on 16 June 1969 spanning an area of about 3,550 km2 (1,371 sq mi). Today it is the third-largest district in Kerala in terms of area
