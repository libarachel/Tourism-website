About Kollam

Kollam is one of Keralaâ€™s fourteen districts famous for its cashew industry. It is known as the cashew capital of the world.The district is blessed with an abundance of natural beauty and has many scenic spots to explore from pristine beaches to tranquil lakes and ancient architecture.Kollam lies on the shores of Ashtamudi Lake. It is the southern gateway to Backwaters of Kerala and is a prominent tourist destination. Thirty percent of this district is covered by Ashtamudi Lake. The eight hour boat trip between Kollam and Alappuzha is the best way to explore the Kerala backwaters. It is also very famous for cashew processing and coir manufacturing.
